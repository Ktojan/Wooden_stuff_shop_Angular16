Here you can see some stuff, maden in the amateur workshop during 2022-2023. It's not bad, but not awesome. So-so, I can say. Normal, even with some fresh ideas and decor.

About the Angular 16 project

It's quite simple store (the design and styling were not the keypoints, as you see), but still includes basic Angular concepts, as well as new-versions tricks, in particular:

  • Combination of Signals and rxjs observables/operators
  • Nested router-outlets and auxiliary routes
  • Router guards
  • Reactive forms with validation
  • Dynamic templates with 'ng-content' as well as shared templates
  • Pipes: async, currency, keyvalue
  • Angular Material elements
  • A lot of one-way, two-ways bindings, template interpolations so on
